<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January">January</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_in_hip_hop_music&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/The_Game_(rapper)" title="The Game (rapper)">The Game</a> states that after his third album, <i><a href="/wiki/L.A.X._(album)" title="L.A.X. (album)" class="mw-redirect">L.A.X.</a></i>, he will retire.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Freekey_Zeekey" title="Freekey Zeekey" class="mw-redirect">Freekey Zeekey</a> states that all is well with <a href="/wiki/The_Diplomats" title="The Diplomats">Dipset</a>, and they are working on new projects.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Rick_Ross_(rapper)" title="Rick Ross (rapper)" class="mw-redirect">Rick Ross</a> says that <i><a href="/wiki/Trilla" title="Trilla">Trilla</a></i> will be one of the best albums of 2008, and that he and <a href="/wiki/Birdman_(rapper)" title="Birdman (rapper)">Birdman</a> are recording an album together, as well as making a movie together.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
<li><a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a>, <a href="/wiki/Timbaland" title="Timbaland">Timbaland</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mary_J._Blige" title="Mary J. Blige">Mary J.
