<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Events">Events</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_in_hip_hop_music&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Events">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="January">January</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_in_hip_hop_music&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: January">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<ul>
<li><a href="/wiki/Eminem" title="Eminem">Eminem</a>, <a href="/wiki/50_Cent" title="50 Cent">50 Cent</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dr._Dre" title="Dr. Dre">Dr. Dre</a>'s new song "<a href="/wiki/Crack_a_Bottle" title="Crack a Bottle">Crack a Bottle</a>" has leaked on the internet. The song will be featured on <a href="/wiki/Eminem" title="Eminem">Eminem</a>'s <i><a href="/wiki/Relapse_(Eminem_album)" title="Relapse (Eminem album)">Relapse</a></i> as a track not a single. It later reached number one.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jermaine_Dupri" title="Jermaine Dupri">Jermaine Dupri</a> has split from Def Jam.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Bun_B" title="Bun B">Bun B</a> stated there would one last album by <a href="/wiki/UGK" title="UGK">UGK</a> titled <i>4 Life</i>. Bun B stated that the album would have 12 tracks and no guest appearances.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Lupe_Fiasco" title="Lupe Fiasco">Lupe Fiasco</a>, <a href="/wiki/2008_in_hip_hop#November" title="2008 in hip hop" class="mw-redirect">last year</a> stated he would be retiring and ending his career with a 3 disc album entitled <i>LupE.N.D</i> but now he said he is not going to retire instead, he's planning to release three albums over the next year and a half.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
</ul>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="February">February</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_in_hip_hop_music&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: February">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
