<p><i><b>Lōc-ed After Dark</b></i> is the debut album by rapper, <a href="/wiki/Tone_L%C5%8Dc" title="Tone Lōc">Tone Lōc</a>. The album was released in 1989 for <a href="/wiki/Delicious_Vinyl_Records" title="Delicious Vinyl Records" class="mw-redirect">Delicious Vinyl Records</a> and was a huge success, making it to #1 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200"><i>Billboard</i> 200</a> the week of the April 15 and #3 on the Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop album charts. It featured three hit singles — "<a href="/wiki/Wild_Thing_(Tone_L%C5%8Dc_song)" title="Wild Thing (Tone Lōc song)">Wild Thing</a>", "<a href="/wiki/Funky_Cold_Medina" title="Funky Cold Medina">Funky Cold Medina</a>", and "<a href="/wiki/I_Got_It_Goin%27_On" title="I Got It Goin' On">I Got It Goin' On</a>" — and was certified Double Platinum by the <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_America" title="Recording Industry Association of America">RIAA</a>.</p>
<p><i>Lōc-ed After Dark'</i>s album cover is based on <a href="/wiki/Donald_Byrd" title="Donald Byrd">Donald Byrd</a>'s 1963 record, <i><a href="/wiki/A_New_Perspective" title="A New Perspective">A New Perspective</a></i>.</p>
