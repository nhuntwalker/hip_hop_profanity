<p><i><b>Tru 2 da Game</b></i> is the fourth <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album" class="mw-redirect">studio album</a> released by rap group, <a href="/wiki/TRU_(group)" title="TRU (group)">TRU</a>. It was released February 18, 1997 for <a href="/wiki/No_Limit_Records" title="No Limit Records">No Limit Records</a> and was produced by <a href="/wiki/Beats_By_the_Pound" title="Beats By the Pound" class="mw-redirect">Beats By the Pound</a> . The album found a large amount of success, peaking at #8 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200">Billboard 200</a> and #2 on the <a href="/wiki/Top_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Albums" title="Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums">Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums</a>. A single from the album enititled "<a href="/wiki/I_Always_Feel_Like" title="I Always Feel Like">I Always Feel Like</a>" also found some success, peaking at #71 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100">Billboard Hot 100</a>, #11 on the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Rap_Singles" title="Hot Rap Singles" class="mw-redirect">Hot Rap Singles</a> and #42 on the <a href="/wiki/Hot_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Singles_%26_Tracks" title="Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Singles &amp; Tracks" class="mw-redirect">Hot R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Singles &amp; Tracks</a>. The same year the album was rereleased with the song "Smoking Green" removed. The rereleased version included three new songs, a remixed version of "FEDz", a C-Murder solo song "Eyes Of A Killa" and a Silkk The Shocker solo song "Pimp Shit." FEDz samples <a href="/wiki/Aaliyah" title="Aaliyah">Aaliyah</a>'s hit single "<a href="/wiki/If_Your_Girl_Only_Knew" title="If Your Girl Only Knew">If Your Girl Only Knew</a>". The album was certified <a href="/wiki/Music_recording_sales_certification" title="Music recording sales certification">2x Platinum</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_America" title="Recording Industry Association of America">RIAA</a>, on October 2, 1997, and remains the group's most successful album.</p>
