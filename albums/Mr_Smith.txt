<p><i><b>Mr. Smith</b></i> is the sixth <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album" class="mw-redirect">studio album</a> by the <a href="/wiki/Rapper" title="Rapper" class="mw-redirect">rapper</a> <a href="/wiki/LL_Cool_J" title="LL Cool J">LL Cool J</a>, released in 1995. After the commercially disappointing <i><a href="/wiki/14_Shots_to_the_Dome" title="14 Shots to the Dome">14 Shots to the Dome</a></i>, it was a success, being certified 2x Platinum. Three top 10 hits were released from the album, "<a href="/wiki/Hey_Lover" title="Hey Lover">Hey Lover</a>", "<a href="/wiki/Doin%27_It_(LL_Cool_J_song)" title="Doin' It (LL Cool J song)">Doin' It</a>", and "<a href="/wiki/Loungin" title="Loungin">Loungin</a>". Unlike his previous release, which focused on hardcore rap, this album concentrates on the ballads for which he is now famous.</p>
