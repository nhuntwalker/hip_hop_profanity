<p><i><b>Black Sunday</b></i> is the second album by rap group <a href="/wiki/Cypress_Hill" title="Cypress Hill">Cypress Hill</a>. It was released on July 20, 1993. It debuted at number one on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200">Billboard 200</a> selling 261,000 copies in its first week,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> recording the highest <a href="/wiki/Soundscan" title="Soundscan" class="mw-redirect">Soundscan</a> for a rap group at the time. The album went <a href="/wiki/Triple_platinum" title="Triple platinum" class="mw-redirect">Triple platinum</a> in the U.S. with 3.4 million units sold.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> Black Sunday is also seen as a hip hop classic and possibly the group's best material to date.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Album_information">Album information</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Black_Sunday_(Cypress_Hill_album)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Album information">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first single, "<a href="/wiki/Insane_in_the_Brain" title="Insane in the Brain">Insane in the Brain</a>," became a <a href="/wiki/Crossover_(music)" title="Crossover (music)">crossover</a> hit, starting a following among rock audiences. A censored version of the album was also made which removes the song "A To The K".</p>
<p>The songs "Hits from the Bong" and "I Wanna Get High" were used in the movie <i><a href="/wiki/How_High" title="How High">How High</a></i>. "I Wanna Get High" was also featured in the vampire junkie film "The Addiction." "Hits from the Bong" was also heard in the film <a href="/wiki/Hall_Pass" title="Hall Pass">Hall Pass</a>. "Hand on the Glock" is a re-recorded version of the track "Hand on the Pump", from the debut album <i><a href="/wiki/Cypress_Hill_(album)" title="Cypress Hill (album)">Cypress Hill</a></i>. The booklet to the album contains 19 facts about the history of hemp and the positive attributes of cannabis.</p>
<p>The song "When The Shit Goes Down" was included in the movie <i><a href="/wiki/This_Is_the_End" title="This Is the End">This Is the End</a></i>.</p>
