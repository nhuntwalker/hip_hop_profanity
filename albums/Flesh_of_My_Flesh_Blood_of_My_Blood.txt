<p><i><b>Flesh of My Flesh, Blood of My Blood</b></i> is the second album released in December 22, 1998 by rapper <a href="/wiki/DMX_(rapper)" title="DMX (rapper)">DMX</a>, six months after his debut album <a href="/wiki/It%27s_Dark_and_Hell_is_Hot" title="It's Dark and Hell is Hot" class="mw-redirect">It's Dark and Hell is Hot</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-rs_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rs-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup> It entered the charts at number one (and stayed there for 3 consecutive weeks) with 670,000 units shipped in the first week.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup> The album eventually went <a href="/wiki/Music_recording_sales_certification" title="Music recording sales certification">3x Platinum</a> in the US.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9"><span>[</span>9<span>]</span></a></sup> He is the second rapper to have two albums released in the same calendar year to debut at number one on <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200">Billboard's Top 200</a>, the first being <a href="/wiki/Tupac_Shakur" title="Tupac Shakur">Tupac Shakur</a> with his albums, <i><a href="/wiki/All_Eyez_on_Me" title="All Eyez on Me">All Eyez on Me</a></i> (February 1996) and <i><a href="/wiki/The_Don_Killuminati:_The_7_Day_Theory" title="The Don Killuminati: The 7 Day Theory">The Don Killuminati: The 7 Day Theory</a></i> (November 1996).</p>
