<p><i><b>Murda Muzik</b></i> is the fourth <a href="/wiki/Studio_album" title="Studio album" class="mw-redirect">studio album</a> by the American hip hop duo <a href="/wiki/Mobb_Deep" title="Mobb Deep">Mobb Deep</a>, which was released in April 27, 1999. It features one of the group's best-known and most acclaimed songs, "Quiet Storm." It is also the duo's most commercially successful album to date, for shipping over 1 million copies and was certified Platinum by the RIAA, debuting at #3 on the Billboard 200 charts. <i>Murda Muzik</i> also garnered positive reviews from <i><a href="/wiki/The_Source_(magazine)" title="The Source (magazine)">The Source</a></i> and <a href="/wiki/Allmusic" title="Allmusic" class="mw-redirect">Allmusic</a>, among others. A censored version of the album, titled <i>Mobb Muzik</i>, was released simultaneously.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>In October 2013, the album was certified 2x Platinum by the RIAA for shipments of two million copies.</p>
