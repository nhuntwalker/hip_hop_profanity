<p>"<b>Let's Get It Started</b>" is a song by American hip hop group <a href="/wiki/The_Black_Eyed_Peas" title="The Black Eyed Peas">The Black Eyed Peas</a>, released as a single in <a href="/wiki/2004_in_radio" title="2004 in radio">2004</a>. The track was the fourth single released from the band's 2003 album <i><a href="/wiki/Elephunk" title="Elephunk">Elephunk</a></i>, and originally appeared on the album under the title "<b>Let's Get Retarded</b>". This version was included as a bonus track on the 2004 re-issue of the album.</p>
<p>The album version was originally only re-worked for its use in promotion for the <a href="/wiki/2004_NBA_Playoffs" title="2004 NBA Playoffs">2004 NBA Playoffs</a> on <a href="/wiki/American_Broadcasting_Company" title="American Broadcasting Company">ABC</a> in April 2004,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> however the new version was so well-received that it was later given an official single release two months later.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup> The song was later included on the soundtrack for the 2006 romantic comedy <i><a href="/wiki/It%27s_a_Boy_Girl_Thing" title="It's a Boy Girl Thing">It's a Boy Girl Thing</a></i>, the 2004 comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Harold_%26_Kumar_Go_to_White_Castle" title="Harold &amp; Kumar Go to White Castle">Harold &amp; Kumar Go to White Castle</a></i> and the 2010 comedy <i><a href="/wiki/Hot_Tub_Time_Machine" title="Hot Tub Time Machine">Hot Tub Time Machine</a></i>. The song is featured during the opening credits of the 2004 motion picture <i><a href="/wiki/Barbershop_2:_Back_in_Business" title="Barbershop 2: Back in Business">Barbershop 2: Back in Business</a></i> (although it is not included in <a href="/wiki/Barbershop_2:_Back_in_Business_(soundtrack)" title="Barbershop 2: Back in Business (soundtrack)">the film's soundtrack album</a>). It was featured in the film <i><a href="/wiki/White_Chicks" title="White Chicks">White Chicks</a></i>. It was used in UK advertisements for the game <i><a href="/wiki/The_Urbz:_Sims_in_the_City" title="The Urbz: Sims in the City">The Urbz: Sims in the City</a></i>. The song was remixed for The Black Eyed Peas' fifth studio album <i><a href="/wiki/The_E.N.D." title="The E.N.D." class="mw-redirect">The E.N.D.</a></i> as "Let's Get Re-Started", it was also featured on their remix album <i><a href="/wiki/The_E.N.D._Summer_2010_Canadian_Invasion_Tour:_Remix_Collection" title="The E.N.D. Summer 2010 Canadian Invasion Tour: Remix Collection" class="mw-redirect">The E.N.D. Summer 2010 Canadian Invasion Tour: Remix Collection</a></i> The chorus was performed by <a href="/wiki/%22Weird_Al%22_Yankovic" title="&quot;Weird Al&quot; Yankovic">"Weird Al" Yankovic</a> in his polka medley "<a href="/wiki/Polkarama" title="Polkarama" class="mw-redirect">Polkarama</a>" from his 2006 album <i><a href="/wiki/Straight_Outta_Lynwood" title="Straight Outta Lynwood">Straight Outta Lynwood</a></i>.</p>
<p>"Let's Get It Started" won <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Rap_Performance_by_a_Duo_or_Group" title="Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group">Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2005_Grammy_Awards" title="2005 Grammy Awards" class="mw-redirect">2005 Grammy Awards</a>, and was also nominated for <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Record_of_the_Year" title="Grammy Award for Record of the Year">Record of the Year</a> and <a href="/wiki/Grammy_Award_for_Best_Rap_Song" title="Grammy Award for Best Rap Song">Best Rap Song</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Music_video">Music video</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Let%27s_Get_It_Started&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Music video">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The music video takes place in a nighttime setting in an urban area. It features The Black Eyed Peas, particularly <a href="/wiki/Will.i.am" title="Will.i.am">will.i.am</a>, performing energetic dances while singing the song, while varying objects in the background (including a grand piano) smash against the ground in real-time, slow and fast motion, and in reverse. The choruses feature the band on stage singing to a large crowd.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Chart_performance">Chart performance</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Let%27s_Get_It_Started&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Chart performance">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>"Let's Get It Started" was the first song to sell 500,000 downloads in the United States meaning that it was once the best-selling digital song of all time. "Let's Get It Started" has sold 3,058,195 copies as of October 2012.</p>
<p>The video went to #34 on "MuchMusic's 50 Most Controversial Videos" for its original song title.</p>
