<p><i><b>Cypress Hill</b></i> is the self-titled debut album of <a href="/wiki/Cypress_Hill" title="Cypress Hill">Cypress Hill</a>, released on August 13, 1991. It was critically and commercially successful, getting certified double Platinum by the <a href="/wiki/RIAA" title="RIAA" class="mw-redirect">RIAA</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> The album is broken down track-by-track by <a href="/wiki/Cypress_Hill" title="Cypress Hill">Cypress Hill</a> in Brian Coleman's book <i><a href="/wiki/Check_the_Technique:_Liner_Notes_for_Hip-Hop_Junkies" title="Check the Technique: Liner Notes for Hip-Hop Junkies" class="mw-redirect">Check the Technique</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

<p>Steve Huey of <a href="/wiki/Allmusic" title="Allmusic" class="mw-redirect">Allmusic</a> calls Cypress Hill's debut "a sonic blueprint that would become one of the most widely copied in hip-hop."<sup id="cite_ref-Allmusic_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Allmusic-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
<p>In 1998, the album was selected as one of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Source_(magazine)" title="The Source (magazine)">The Source's</a></i> 100 Best Rap Albums.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8"><span>[</span>8<span>]</span></a></sup> The album was included in the book <a href="/wiki/1001_Albums_You_Must_Hear_Before_You_Die" title="1001 Albums You Must Hear Before You Die">1001 Albums You Must Hear Before You Die</a>.</p>
<p><i><a href="/wiki/Rolling_Stone" title="Rolling Stone">Rolling Stone</a></i> called it "an album that is innovative and engaging in spite of its hard-core messages."<sup id="cite_ref-Rolling_Stone_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Rolling_Stone-5"><span>[</span>5<span>]</span></a></sup></p>

</ul>
<ul>
<li>Ranked #57 in <i><a href="/wiki/Spin_(magazine)" title="Spin (magazine)">Spin Magazine</a></i><span style="padding-left:0.1em;">'</span>s "90 Greatest Albums of the '90s".<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10"><span>[</span>10<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Included in <i><a href="/wiki/Q_(magazine)" title="Q (magazine)" class="mw-redirect">Q Magazine</a></i><span style="padding-left:0.1em;">'</span>s "90 Best Albums Of The 1990s".<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11"><span>[</span>11<span>]</span></a></sup></li>
