<div class="hatnote">This article is about the rap album.  For the warning sign, see <a href="/wiki/Beware_of_the_dog" title="Beware of the dog">Beware of the dog</a>.  For the WWF pay-per-view event, see <a href="/wiki/In_Your_House_8:_Beware_of_Dog" title="In Your House 8: Beware of Dog">In Your House 8: Beware of Dog</a>.</div>
<p><i><b>Beware of Dog</b></i> is the debut album by <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">American</a> <a href="/wiki/Rapping" title="Rapping">rapper</a> <a href="/wiki/Bow_Wow_(rapper)" title="Bow Wow (rapper)">Lil' Bow Wow</a>. It was released on September 26, 2000 on <a href="/wiki/So_So_Def" title="So So Def" class="mw-redirect">So So Def</a> and <a href="/wiki/Columbia_Records" title="Columbia Records">Columbia Records</a>. Producer <a href="/wiki/Jermaine_Dupri" title="Jermaine Dupri">Jermaine Dupri</a> and songwriter/producer <a href="/wiki/Bryan-Michael_Cox" title="Bryan-Michael Cox">Bryan-Michael Cox</a> worked with Lil' Bow Wow for the album.</p>
<p>The lead single was "<a href="/wiki/Bounce_with_Me" title="Bounce with Me">Bounce with Me</a>". The song was originally released on <a href="/wiki/Big_Momma%27s_House_(soundtrack)" title="Big Momma's House (soundtrack)">the soundtrack</a> for the film <i><a href="/wiki/Big_Momma%27s_House" title="Big Momma's House">Big Momma's House</a></i>. The album debuted at number eight on the US <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200"><i>Billboard</i> 200</a> chart, with 101,000 copies sold in the first week released.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3"><span>[</span>3<span>]</span></a></sup> The album was certified 2x platinum by the <a href="/wiki/Recording_Industry_Association_of_America" title="Recording Industry Association of America">Recording Industry Association of America</a> (RIAA) on March 5, 2001. The album reached platinum status in <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>, selling over 100,000 copies.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4"><span>[</span>4<span>]</span></a></sup></p>
