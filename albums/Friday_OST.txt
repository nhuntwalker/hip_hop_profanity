<p><i><b>Friday</b></i> is the soundtrack album for the 1995 comedy film <i><a href="/wiki/Friday_(1995_film)" title="Friday (1995 film)">Friday</a></i>. It was released on April 11, 1995, on CD and cassette through <a href="/wiki/Sony_Music" title="Sony Music" class="mw-redirect">Sony Music</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Epic_Records" title="Epic Records">Epic Records</a> and consisted of hip hop and R&amp;B music. The soundtrack reached No. 1 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_200" title="Billboard 200">Billboard 200</a>, where it held the position for two weeks, and the <a href="/wiki/Top_R%26B/Hip-Hop_Albums" title="Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums">Top R&amp;B/Hip-Hop Albums</a> chart for six weeks. It also spawned the successful <a href="/wiki/Dr._Dre" title="Dr. Dre">Dr. Dre</a> single "<a href="/wiki/Keep_Their_Heads_Ringin%27" title="Keep Their Heads Ringin'">Keep Their Heads Ringin'</a>", which made it to No. 10 on the <a href="/wiki/Billboard_Hot_100" title="Billboard Hot 100">Billboard Hot 100</a> and No. 1 on the <a href="/wiki/Hot_Rap_Tracks" title="Hot Rap Tracks" class="mw-redirect">Hot Rap Tracks</a> chart. The album was certified 2x platinum on June 4, 1996, while the single, "Keep Their Heads Ringin'", was certified gold on May 10, 1995.</p>
